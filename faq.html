About the Camp

Soccer Player Development delivers professional soccer camps in Saskatoon for boys and girls ages 6–18. Our program features licensed coaches, structured training sessions aligned with a proven developmental philosophy, and a fun, competitive environment designed to help every player grow.

The camp provides 5 days of professional coaching (or 1 day for PD Day camps) using a proven soccer development methodology. Water is supplied during breaks throughout the day. Participants receive a certified diploma upon completing the full week. Pricing and full inclusions will be announced when registration opens — join the waitlist to be first to know.

Boys and girls in the U9, U11, U13, and U15 age groups are welcome at our camps. Players are grouped by age and assessed by our coaches to ensure they're placed at the right level — challenged enough to grow, but supported to succeed.

Registration & Payment

Registration is not open yet — we're building the waitlist now. Join the waitlist on this website and we'll email you the moment registration opens. When it does, everything will be handled online through our registration portal.

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • 90+ days before camp: 75% refund
  • 31–89 days before: 50% refund

  • Full refunds are issued if the camp is cancelled by Soccer Player Development.

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover through our secure online registration system. Payment details will be provided when registration opens.

At Camp

Required items:

  • Soccer cleats
  • Personal water bottle
  • Packed lunch
  • Sunscreen
  • Camp training kit (details TBA)

Recommended: shin guards, snacks, isotonic drinks.

Participants will be required to purchase a camp training kit. Full details — including what's included and where to order — will be provided when registration opens. Stay tuned!

The Summer Camp runs 5 days, Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM:

  • 8:45 AMArrival & check-in
  • 9:00 AMGroup organization & warm-up
  • 9:30 AMTechnical training (until 12:00 PM)
  • 12:00 PMLunch break (until 12:30 PM)
  • 12:30 PMTactical training (until 1:30 PM)
  • 1:30 PMMatch play & competition
  • 3:00 PMDeparture
  • Day 5Closing ceremony & diploma at ~2:30 PM

Schedule is subject to minor adjustments for weather or special activities.

Parents are warmly welcomed to observe training sessions. We simply ask that you remain in the designated viewing area to avoid disrupting the flow of sessions and to allow players to stay focused on their coaches.

Groups are initially organized by age category (U9, U11, U13, U15). On the first day of camp, our coaches conduct a brief skills assessment to ensure every player is placed at the right level. Reassignments may occur during camp to ensure the best fit for each player's development.

Coaches & Safety

Our camps are led by Keisuke Johnston (Head Coach) and Jacob Houmphanh, both licensed soccer coaches with extensive experience developing youth players. All coaches are vetted, certified, and deeply committed to player development. Learn more about our coaches →

We maintain a maximum ratio of 1 coach per 12 players, ensuring every player receives the individual attention they need to improve. This small group structure is a cornerstone of our program and sets us apart.

A certified first aid attendant will be available on-site throughout the camp. Campers must maintain their own personal medical coverage. Soccer Player Development provides supplementary insurance as a secondary layer of coverage, though campers remain primarily responsible for their own medical expenses. Full insurance details will be provided at registration.

Player safety is our top priority. During adverse weather, campers are moved to a designated shelter area immediately. If a session must be cancelled after it has begun due to severe weather, parents will be notified to pick up players. Any major schedule impacts due to weather will be communicated promptly.
